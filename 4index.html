How to Avoid
  • Practice self-care and prioritized your well-being.
  • Set boundaries and maintain a healthy work-life balance.
  • Delegate tasks and ask for help when needed.
  • Manage your time effectively to avoid overwhelming workloads.
  • Cultivate a strong support system of friends and family.
  • Engage in regular exercise and stress-reducing activities.
  • Develop healthy coping mechanisms like mindfulness or journaling.
  • Take regular breaks to rest and recharge.
  • Regularly reassess your goals and adjust as needed.
