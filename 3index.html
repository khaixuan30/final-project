Burnout'sCauses
  • High workload and excessive job demands
  • Long working hours and lack of work-life balance
  • Chronic stress and pressure to meet deadlines
  • Lack of control or autonomy in work
  • Insufficient support or recognition from colleagues or superiors
  • Conflict or difficult interpersonal relationships at work
  • Monotony or lack of meaningfulness in tasks
  • Personal perfectionism and high self-imposed expectations
  • Poor organizational culture or lack of values alignment
  • Inadequate self-care and neglecting personal well-being
